We're kicking things off with a new music release!

'Match My BOUNCE!' is a track that I collaborated with the wonderful PTB10 and exorax on just last month. As part of All Nighter Vol. 9, a charity event in aid of the National Alliance for Eating Disorders (USA) run by the online artist community artbyFORM, we produced this funky little number in the space of just 24 hours!

Huge thanks, of course, go to PTB for his production wizardry and exorax for their music theory know-how, without which this track would have never come to fruition. If I start posting music progress here, you'll see that I can't make anything this good on my own just yet...
